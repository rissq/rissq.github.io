About

This is the website for the project RISSQ (R Infrastructure for Statistical Standards on Quality). The project was started by the author of the SixSigma R package author, Emilio L. Cano1.

  1. Emilio L. Cano, Statistician. Associate Professor at the Faculty of Law and Social Sciences of the University of Castilla-la Mancha (UCLM), as well as research scientist at the Institute for Regional Development (IDR) of the UCLM. Emilio is member of the national standardisation technical committee TC66 - statistical methods at UNE (Spanish member of ISO) and trainer at the Spanish association for quality (AEC). http://blog.uclm.es/emiliolcano [return]
